k1 - MUNNAR PERIYAR ALLEPPY 6 Days 5 Hotel Nights

Visits :

Day 1 - Kochi
Arrive at Kochi, report to coach and proceed to the hotel. Enjoy live Kathakali dance performance in the evening






dinner
Day 2 - Kochi – Munnar
Visit St. Francis Church, Jewish Synagogue (Subject to operation) and Dutch Palace. Take a Harbour Cruise Ride to see the Chinese Fishing Nets. Later Proceed to Munnar. Arrive at Munnar in the evening.
Extra Topping : Coconut Water at Dutch Palace. Tea / Coffee / Soft Drink at Hilda en-route from Kochi to Munnar.
breakfast lunch  dinner
Day 3 - Munnar
Enjoy speed boat ride at Mattupetty Dam. View Tea Garden, visit Echo Point and the Eravikulam (Rajamalai) National Park (subject to operation) OR Tata Tea Museum.
Extra Topping : Pineapple slice at Echo Point. Paan at Munnar. Ice Cream / Tea / Coffee / Soft Drink at Eravikulam National Park.

breakfast lunch dinner

Day 4 - Munnar – Periyar (Thekkady)
Proceed to Periyar. On arrival free time for shopping at Kumily Spice Village.
Extra Topping : Tea / Coffee / Soft Drink enroute to Periyar.


breakfast lunch dinner
Day 5 - Periyar – Alleppey (Houseboat)
Visit Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary by boat in Periyar Lake (Subject to Operation). Proceed to Alleppey. Arrive in Alleppey. On arrival houseboat check in. The most predominant way to experience the Kerala with Kerala houseboats. Enjoy the famous Kerala Backwaters ride.
breakfast lunch dinner
Day 6 - Alleppey – Departure from Kochi
Proceed to Kochi. Board the flight /Train to your hometown. Tour Concludes.



breakfast
