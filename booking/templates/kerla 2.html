k2 - KERALA KANYAKUMARI ECONOMY 7 Days 6 Hotel Nights

Visits :

Day 1 - Trivendrum
Arrive at Trivendrum, report to coach and proceed to hotel . relax little bit read instruction of trip







dinner
Day 2 - Trivandrum - Kanyakumari - Trivandrum
Today early morning proceed to Kanyakumari. On arrival visit Kanyakumari Mandir, Tri-Samudra Milan and Gandhi Mandapam. Take a ferry ride to Swami Vivekanand Rock Memorial to see the meditation hall and smarak.
Extra Topping : Coconut water at Kanyakumari. Dalwada at Kovalam beach. Tea/Coffee/ Soft Drink en-route to Trivendrum
breakfast lunch  dinner
Day 3 - Trivandrum – Padmanabh – Alleppey – Periyar (Thekkady)
Enjoy speed boat ride at Mattupetty Dam. View Tea Garden, visit Echo Point and the Eravikulam (Rajamalai) National Park (subject to operation) OR Tata Tea Museum.
Extra Topping : Pineapple slice at Echo Point. Paan at Munnar. Ice Cream / Tea / Coffee / Soft Drink at Eravikulam National Park.

breakfast lunch dinner

Day 4 - Munnar – Periyar (Thekkady)
Today morning visit beautiful Padmanabhaswamy temple – dedicated to Lord Vishnu is the richest temple in the world and renowned for its beauty and grandeur. Later Proceed to Periyar, en-route enjoy Backwater Ride from Alleppey to Nedumudi. Arrive at Periyar in the evening.
Extra Topping : Banana chips at Padmanabha Swami temple. Tea / Coffee / Soft Drink en-route to Alleppey. Tea / Coffee / Soft Drink en-route to Periyar.
breakfast lunch dinner
Day 5 - Periyar – Alleppey (Houseboat)
Visit Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary by boat in Periyar Lake (Subject to Operation). Proceed to Alleppey. Arrive in Alleppey. On arrival houseboat check in. The most predominant way to experience the Kerala with Kerala houseboats. Enjoy the famous Kerala Backwaters ride.



breakfast lunch dinner
Day 6 Munnar - Kochi
Arrive at Kochi. Visit St. Francis Church, Jewish Synagogue (Subject to operation) and Dutch Palace. Take a Harbour Cruise ride to see Chinese Fishing Nets. Free time for shopping. Enjoy get-together with your travel companions in the evening.
Extra Topping : Tea / Coffee / Soft drink en-route to Kochi.


breakfast

Day 7 - Alleppey – Departure from Kochi
Proceed to Kochi. Board the flight /Train to your hometown. Tour Concludes.
breakfast
